about this item:

This year, Edison Chen's Clot and Jordan Brand will collaborate to make an Air Jordan 5 Low. Clot has released a number of shoe collaborations with Nike and Jordan throughout the years.

Chen and Clot are known for drawing inspiration for their collaborations from Chinese culture, and this Air Jordan 5 Low is no exception. The shoe has a black base with red highlights on the heel, tongue, and sock liner, as well as jade elements throughout, most notably the jade beads on the lace stays. Chinese characters can be seen on the lateral heel, while Clot labeling can be found on the back of the heel, along with jade lace locks and a transparent outsole.
