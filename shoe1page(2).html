Billie Eilish, a youthful, modern artist, adores and appreciates the classics, especially sneaker legends like the Air Force 1. Five massive mid-foot straps — inspired by the Alpha Force Low and Air Trainer 3 — hide the laces, giving the design a distinctive and bold look.
